About Us

Where Expectations Soar and Dreams Take Flight

Brighton College is an Australian Registereed Training Organisation (RTO) opearting under the National Vocational Educationa and Training (VET) Quality Framework (VQF). This is a regulated framework that is adminstrered by the Australian Skills Qualioty Authority (ASQA).
Brighton College was first registered to provide educational services in 2016 and is extended its educational services to International Students in 2017 at Brisbane, Queensland.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students with the necessary skill set to excel in the current workforce. We are dedicated to supporting our students on their journey towards achieving their goals and aspirations by fostering practical and academic skills, knowledge, and capability. At Brighton, our commitment lies in providing professional adult education within a relaxed and supportive environment. Our aim is to cultivate highly skilled and job-ready graduates. Our approach to success revolves around:

Empowering students through education and training.
Offering progressive and innovating qualifications that inspire students to thrive for excellence.
Assisting sudents in achieving their personal best in gaining a practical Education.
Ensuring small class sizes to provide individual attention to each student.
Enlisting qualified, experienced, and dedicated trainers who possess extensive knowledge and
expertise in their respective fields.
