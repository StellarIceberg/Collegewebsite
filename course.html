ICT50220
Diploma of Information Technology (Cyber Security)

CRICOS Course Code: 105308G

About this course

This qualification reflects the role of individuals in a variety of information and communications technology (ICT) roles who have established specialised skills in a technical ICT function. Individuals in these roles carry out moderately complex tasks in specialist fields, working independently, as part of a team or leading deliverables with others. They may apply their skills across a wide range of industries, business functions and departments,or as a business owner (sole trader/contractor).


Admission: Entry and Criteria

1. Age:

Students must be a minimum of 19 years old.

2. Academic Qualifications:

Completion of the equivalent of year 12 with exceptional academic standing is required.

3. English Language Proficiency:

Provide evidence of an IELTS Academic score of 6.0 or equivalent, with test results no older than 1 year.

4. Course Entry Interview:

Participation in a rigorous course entry interview to demonstrate exceptional suitability and a strong passion for the field of IT.

Exemptions from providing evidence of English language competence may be granted if you can present well-documented evidence of meeting any of the following criteria:
Education in an English-speaking country: Have received education for a minimum of 7 years in an English-speaking country.
Completion of a Certificate IV level course: Successfully completed at least 9 months of a Certificate IV level course in an Australian Registered Training Organization (RTO) with outstanding performance.
Advanced English Placement Test: Achieved a superior score on an advanced English Placement Test.
Completion of an intensive foundation course: Successfully completed an intensive foundation course in Australia focused on advanced English language skills.
High School education in English: Completed entire high school education in English Language with outstanding grades. Please note that alternative English language tests such as PTE and TOEFL can be accepted, provided you can provide results that are confirmed to be equivalent to IELTS 6.0 or higher.
